FAQs ⋮

Finding nearby clinics ▾
Open Locate Hospitals from the sidebar, then search by name or area, or browse the map for clinics close to you.
Understanding risk scores ▾
Risk scores combine your symptoms, history, and habits into a likelihood estimate. They support a conversation with a doctor — they don't replace one.
How the AI makes predictions ▾
The Symptom Checker uses a model trained on common conditions in Nigeria, weighted by your age, weight, and medical history.
Emergency contacts ▾
Use the Urgent Care Contacts panel on this page to call Lagos State Emergency Services directly.
Switching to Yoruba ▾
Look for the English / Yoruba toggle near the Symptom Checker and Health Hub cards to switch the assistant's language.
